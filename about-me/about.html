About Me

I'm a Visual Designer that loves to learn new skills and create beautiful designs that help others share their passions with the world. I start with the concept I want to use, then draw the sketch that will embody the project, and then move on to creating it digitally.

Before learning how to wireframe and use Adobe Creative Suite, I was working in Finance. I began learning web design online so I could start freelancing as a side hustle. As soon as I learned about color iconography and typefaces I was hooked and I've been seeking out visual design opportunities ever since. I love knowing that I can help businesses reach their ideal customers with clean, user-friendly designs that meet their needs on any platform.

You can usually find me watching a documentary or learning something new on Youtube. I love listening to music and audiobooks
